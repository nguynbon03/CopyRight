How We Protect Our Content

All original content published on CelebPost is protected under DMCA. Here is our enforcement process.

1

Detect Infringement

We monitor for unauthorised use of our articles, photos, and videos across all major platforms.

2

Collect Evidence

Screenshots, timestamps, and URLs are archived as proof of ownership and prior publication.

3

File DMCA Notice

A formal takedown notice is submitted to the hosting platform citing 17 U.S.C. § 512.

4

Content Removed

Platforms remove infringing content within 24–72 hours (Facebook) or 1–2 weeks (Google).
